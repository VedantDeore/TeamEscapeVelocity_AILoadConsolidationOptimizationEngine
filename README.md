An **AI-powered logistics optimization system** that intelligently consolidates shipments to maximize vehicle utilization, reduce transportation costs, and minimize carbon emissions.

This project was built as a **hackathon prototype using open-source tools**.

---

# 📌 Background

Logistics networks frequently transport shipments using **partially filled vehicles** due to inefficient load planning and lack of consolidation intelligence.

Shipments that could be **combined into a single vehicle** are often transported separately, leading to:

- Underutilized vehicle capacity
- Higher operational costs
- Increased number of trips
- Higher fuel consumption
- Increased carbon emissions

Manual load planning systems struggle to identify consolidation opportunities across **large shipment volumes and complex logistics networks**.

---

# ❗ Problem Statement

Current load planning processes lack **AI-driven consolidation capabilities**.

Key inefficiencies include:

- 🚚 Underutilized vehicle capacity  
- 📦 Poor shipment grouping  
- ⛽ Increased fuel consumption  
- 💰 Higher transportation costs  
- 🌍 Increased carbon emissions  

There is currently **no automated system capable of intelligently identifying optimal shipment consolidation strategies** across logistics networks.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

The **AI Load Consolidation Optimization Engine** analyzes shipment data and automatically groups shipments into optimized vehicle loads.

The system uses **AI-based clustering and optimization algorithms** to determine the best shipment combinations based on:

- Vehicle capacity
- Shipment weight and volume
- Destination proximity
- Delivery constraints

This results in:

- 🚛 Higher vehicle utilization  
- 📉 Reduced number of trips  
- 💰 Lower transportation costs  
- 🌱 Reduced carbon footprint  

---

# 🧠 Key Features

### AI Shipment Consolidation

### Smart Destination Clustering

### Cost Reduction

### Carbon Emission Reduction
